🔥 Biggest deal this week
Hongqing Technology 🇨🇳
China  |  LEO Satellite Manufacturing
$191M
Growth Round
LEO ConstellationSatellite ManufacturingMega-constellation
Hongqing Technology - the satellite manufacturing arm of Chinese launch company Landspace - raised more than 1.3 billion yuan ($191M) on July 2, bringing total financing to over $368M. The round was led by the investment arms of China Construction Bank and ICBC alongside Jinpu Capital, with all five Chinese state banks participating. Hongqing filed the Honghu-3 constellation with the ITU in May 2024 - 10,000 satellites across 160 orbital planes - making it China's third 10,000+ satellite megaconstellation alongside the national Guowang and Shanghai-backed Qianfan programs. This is one of the largest single raises by a Chinese commercial satellite manufacturer on record.
Space-Comm Outreach Angle
China's commercial space sector is maturing fast and actively seeking European credibility. Landspace already operates the Zhuque-2 rocket and Hongqing is building constellation-scale manufacturing capacity. As Chinese companies target European spectrum filings, supply chain partnerships, and commercial customers, Space-Comm Expo Europe 2027 at ExCeL London is the signal event that puts them in the room with ESA, European operators, and defence procurement. Worth watching as a future international exhibitor or delegation presence.
